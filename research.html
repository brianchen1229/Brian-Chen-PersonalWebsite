Research

My research experience primarily focuses on Machine Learning and Deep Learning. Currently, I am working on Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Models (DDPM) at Academia Sinica, where we develop a Bayesian framework that integrates catalytic priors derived from classical statistical models to enhance predictive stability and uncertainty quantification. Additionally, I also conducted research on empirical asset pricing in the cryptocurrency market, comparing multiple machine learning methods—such as tree-based models, gradient boosting, Support Vector Regression (SVR), K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN), and neural networks—to construct effective factor investing strategies.
